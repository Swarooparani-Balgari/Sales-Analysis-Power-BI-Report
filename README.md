## Sales Analysis Report (Power BI)

### Objective
Analyze sales data to evaluate regional performance, category trends, customer contribution, and overall profitability using Power BI.

### Tool Used
- Power BI

### Analysis Performed
- Region-wise sales, profit, and discount analysis
- Category-wise and year-wise sales trend analysis
- Identification of top customers and high-performing cities
- KPI-based performance summary

### Key Insights
- High-sales regions do not always translate to high profit margins, indicating pricing or cost inefficiencies.
- Technology category is the primary revenue driver compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.
- Year-over-year sales show consistent growth, indicating stable demand.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of customers, highlighting dependency risk.

### KPIs Tracked
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Discount
- Shipping Cost
- Record Count
